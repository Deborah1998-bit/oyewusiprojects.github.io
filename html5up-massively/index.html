Unilever Canteen Ordering System Project

This was a unique project that focuses on Implementing an Innovative Canteen Ordering System, Unilever is a British Dutch FMCG company headquarter in London England Unilever is one of the oldest FMCG companies, and its products are available in around 190 countries. In its UK offices, Unilever has around 1500 employees which are spread across 12 floors. They have 2 canteens to cater to these 1500 employees. Each canteen can seat around 150 employees at a time.This project aims to improve the lunch experience for employees by implementing a web-based canteen ordering system. The system allows employees to pre-order their meals online, reducing waiting times and minimizing food waste. Key features includes Online ordering, Menu management, Order processing, Delivery system, Payment Integration and Feedback mechanism. The system is expected to enhance employee satisfaction, improve efficiency, and reduce the environmenetal impact of food waste.
